The Ultimate Rice and Beans Recipe

Rice and Beans, or beans and rice, is a category of dishes from many cultures around the world, whereby the staple foods of rice and beans are combined in some manner. The grain and legume combination provides several important nutrients and many calories, and both food are widely available. The beans are usually seasoned, while the rice may be plain or seasoned. If the beans do not top the rice, the two components may be mixed together, separated on the plate, or served separately.

Also, as the name implies, rice and beans are the main ingredients, but you can add various spices, vegetables, and meats to enhance the flavor. This recipe is a simple yet delicious way to enjoy this classic dish.

Recipe Information

Preparation Time: 5 minutes

Cooking Time: 60 - 90 minutes

Servings: 2 - 3 people

Difficulty Level: Beginner

Ingredients

  • 1 cup beans
  • 2 cups of Rice
  • 1 small Onion diced
  • 6 to 7 cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter or coconut oil

Instructions

  1. Pick the beans, rinse and cook.
  2. Add dicd onions and cover with some water. Leave it to cook for about 40 - 45 minutes under medium-high heat until the beans are almost done. You will know it is almost done when it is almsot soft
  3. Rinse the rice a couple of times until the water is clear and add the rice to the beans and add butter/oil and salt to taste.
  4. Cook for another 20 - 25 minutes until it is properly cooked.
  5. Serve with your favorite sauce, soup or stew.s

Tips & Notes

  • Ensure that your beans are well-picked and clean before you use them. Chewing on rotten beans or stones is not always a fun experience
  • Some beans are full of pesticides, and the water can get dark and nasty. If that is the case, please drain the beans halfway during the cooking time, rinse them, and replace with clean water before continuing the cooking process.
  • I used the long-grain rice, thats why i cooked the rice for about 20 minutes, but you will end up with well-separated grains, which I love. Please follow the cooking instructions for your Rice.
